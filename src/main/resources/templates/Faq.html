frequently asked questions

introduction

what is women rider?

women rider is a dedicated ride-sharing platform designed exclusively for women. we connect female passengers with female drivers to ensure a secure, comfortable, and empowering transportation experience tailored specifically to women’s needs.

how do i book a ride with women rider?

to book a ride, download the women rider app, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, and select your ride preference. confirm your booking, and a nearby female driver will be assigned to you promptly.

what distinguishes women rider from other ride-sharing services?

women rider offers a unique value by exclusively pairing female passengers with female drivers, ensuring a focus on safety and empowerment. our platform emphasizes a supportive environment where women can travel with confidence and ease.

how does women rider ensure the safety of its users?

safety is paramount at women rider. we conduct comprehensive background checks on all female drivers and verify each ride multiple times to meet the highest standards of security. additional safety features include real-time ride tracking, an sos button, and driver ratings.

how can i contact women rider support?

for any inquiries or assistance, you can reach women rider support through the app or via our website’s support section. our dedicated team is available 24/7 to address any questions or issues you may encounter.

what should i do if i leave an item in the vehicle?

if you accidentally leave an item in a vehicle, please contact women rider support through the app or our website as soon as possible. we will assist you in coordinating with the driver to recover your belongings.
