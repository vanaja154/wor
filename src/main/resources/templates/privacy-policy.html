privacy policy

introduction

at women rider (wor), we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access our website and mobile application (collectively, the "platform"). by using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. information we collect

1.1 personal information

we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other details when you register, make payments, or interact with our services.

1.2 location data

we collect location data to provide location-based services, including ride-matching, navigation, and safety features like real-time tracking.

1.3 usage data

we gather data on how you interact with our platform, including ip address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited, to improve our services.

1.4 device information

we collect information about the device you use to access our platform, such as the hardware model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

1.5 payment information

we collect payment details for transactions, such as credit/debit card numbers and billing information. this data is processed by third-party payment processors and not stored on our servers.

2. how we use your information

2.1 service provision

we use your information to provide, maintain, and enhance our services, including ride-sharing, customer support, and personalized recommendations.

2.2 safety and security

your information helps us improve the safety and security of our services, including verifying users and preventing fraud.

2.3 analytics and research

aggregated data is used for analytics and research to understand user behavior and improve our services.

2.4 marketing and communications

with your consent, we may use your information to communicate about promotions, surveys, updates, and other marketing activities.

2.5 compliance with legal obligations

we may use your information to comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

3. information sharing and disclosure

3.1 service providers

we share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing and customer support.

3.2 other users

for ride-sharing, certain information may be shared with other users, including your name, profile photo, and location data during rides.

3.3 legal purposes

we may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.

3.4 business transfers

in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. data security

we implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. however, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. your choices and rights

5.1 access and update

you can access and update your personal information through your account settings.

5.2 data deletion

you may request the deletion of your account and personal information by contacting us, subject to legal and business retention requirements.

5.3 marketing communications

you can opt-out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications.

6. data retention

we retain your personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. international data transfers

your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. by using our services, you consent to such transfers.

8. changes to this privacy policy

we may update this privacy policy periodically. any changes will be posted on this page with the revised "last updated" date. continued use of the platform indicates your acceptance of the changes.

9. contact us

for any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@nuhvin.com.
