our mission

Our mission is to encourage, motivate, and elevate female riders by building a strong community network and providing opportunities for advancement. We aim to:

Foster Community and Support

We create a vibrant and inclusive space where women riders can connect, share experiences, and support one another through events, rides, and meet-ups.

Advocate for Equality

By highlighting the achievements of women riders and advocating for inclusive policies, we work towards greater gender equality and representation in the motorcycling community.

Promote Engagement and Networking

We encourage interaction and networking among women riders to build lasting friendships and professional connections within the motorcycling community.
